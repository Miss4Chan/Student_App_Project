Welcome to STUDENT APP

What is STUDENT APP?

STUDENT APP is an interactive student-oriented map made by a 5-member team of Software Engineering students. The application is constructed on top of the OpenStreetMap API, using the Spring Boot and Thymeleaf technologies, as part of an incremental homework assignment for the Software Design and Architecture course at FINKI - Skopje, winter semester 2022/23.

What does the map do?

This map is a scaled-down version of the more well-known geolocation web services, geared towards helping students orient themselves in the city of Skopje after spending years of their college experience stuck behind a computer monitor at home.

How do I use STUDENT APP?

STUDENT APP is designed to be a simple application with an intuitive interface and every functionality always being just a few clicks away. You can preview details about a certain location by clicking on its pin, create an account and save your favourites, or simply browse locations by their names or addresses.
